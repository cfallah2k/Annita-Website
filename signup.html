Create a premium personal account to enjoy higher limits, exclusive features, and priority support across our African markets.

Required Documents:
  • Valid Government-issued ID (Passport, National ID, or Driver's License)
  • Proof of address (Utility bill or bank statement from the last 3 months)
  • Recent photo (taken within the last 6 months)
  • Proof of income (for higher limits)
  • Phone number verification
  • Email verification
Premium Benefits:
