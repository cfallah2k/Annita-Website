Account Verification Requirements

Please select an account type to see specific verification requirements.

Personal Account Verification

Create a personal account to send money, make payments, and manage your finances securely.

Required Documents:
  • Valid Government-issued ID (Passport, National ID, or Driver's License)
  • Proof of address (Utility bill or bank statement from the last 3 months)
  • Recent photo (taken within the last 6 months)
  • Phone number verification
  • Email verification
Verification Process:
  • Basic identity verification (1-2 business days)
  • Transaction limits: Up to $3,000 per day after verification
  • Enhanced verification available for higher limits

Freelancer/Sole Trader Verification

Perfect for freelancers, independent contractors, and sole proprietors looking to manage business transactions.

Required Documents:
  • All personal account verification documents
  • Business registration certificate (if applicable)
  • Tax identification number
  • Portfolio or business website (recommended)
  • Proof of business activity (contracts or invoices)
Verification Process:
  • Business identity verification (2-3 business days)
  • Transaction limits: Up to $10,000 per day after verification
  • Access to payment links and basic invoice tools

Startup Business Verification

Designed for early-stage companies needing financial services to support rapid growth.

Required Documents:
  • Business registration certificate
  • Articles of incorporation
  • Tax identification number
  • Director ID (passport or national ID)
  • Proof of business address
  • Company bank statement (if available)
  • Shareholder information (for those with >25% ownership)
Verification Process:
  • Full business verification (3-5 business days)
  • Director/owner verification
  • Transaction limits: Up to $50,000 per day after verification
  • Access to multi-user accounts and business analytics

Small to Medium Enterprise Verification

Comprehensive financial solutions for established businesses with multiple employees and regular transaction needs.

Required Documents:
  • All startup verification documents
  • Business financial statements (last 2 years if available)
  • Business plan or company profile
  • Organizational structure
  • Proof of operational business (contracts, invoices)
  • Additional shareholder/director information
  • Business license or industry-specific permits
Verification Process:
  • Enhanced business verification (5-7 business days)
  • Background checks for key personnel
  • Transaction limits: Up to $250,000 per day after full verification
  • Access to bulk payments, API integration, and advanced reporting

Enterprise Business Verification

Premium financial solutions for large corporations with complex transaction needs across multiple countries.

Required Documents:
  • All SME verification documents
  • Audited financial statements (last 3 years)
  • Corporate governance documents
  • Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) declaration
  • Board resolution approving account opening
  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML) policy
  • Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures
  • Parent/subsidiary structure
  • Parent/subsidiary structure documentation
Verification Process:
  • Comprehensive business verification (7-10 business days)
  • Enhanced due diligence
  • Custom transaction limits based on business needs
  • Access to dedicated account manager and custom solutions
  • Multi-currency accounts and global payment solutions

Charity/Non-Profit Verification

Specialized financial services for charities and non-profit organizations with reduced fees.

Required Documents:
  • Charity registration certificate
  • Tax exemption documentation
  • Board members' identification
  • Proof of address for the organization
  • Latest annual report or financial statement
  • Constitution or governing documents
  • Minutes from latest board meeting
Verification Process:
  • Charity verification (3-5 business days)
  • Verification of charitable status
  • Transaction limits: Based on organizational needs
  • Access to reduced-fee donation processing
  • Specialized reporting for supporters and stakeholders

Religious Institution Verification

Tailored financial services for churches, mosques, temples, and other religious organizations.

Required Documents:
  • Religious institution registration certificate
  • Tax exemption documentation
  • Identification of religious leaders or officers
  • Proof of religious institution address
  • Constitution or governing documents
  • Financial statements or budget
Verification Process:
  • Institution verification (3-5 business days)
  • Verification of religious organization status
  • Transaction limits: Based on organizational needs
  • Access to donation and contribution management tools
  • Specialized services for religious organization treasurers
