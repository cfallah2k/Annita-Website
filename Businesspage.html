Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Annita Premium Business Solutions.

What makes Annita Premium different from regular business accounts?

Annita Premium offers advanced features like multi-currency support, higher transaction limits, dedicated account managers, priority support, comprehensive analytics, and specialized tools for business growth that aren't available with regular accounts.

How secure are transactions with Annita Premium?

We use bank-level security with 256-bit encryption, PCI DSS compliance, 3D Secure authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. Premium accounts also get additional security features like IP restrictions and transaction whitelisting.

Can I integrate Annita with my existing website or app?

Yes! We offer easy integration options including APIs, plugins for popular platforms like WordPress and Shopify, and custom integration support for enterprise clients.

What kind of customer support is available for Premium users?

Premium users get 24/7 priority support via phone, email, and live chat with faster response times. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager and technical support specialist.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

Our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with no penalties.
