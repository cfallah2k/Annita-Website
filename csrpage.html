Corporate Social Responsibility

At Annita, we believe in using our platform to create meaningful change across Africa. Our CSR initiatives focus on empowering communities, fostering digital inclusion, and driving sustainable development.

Our CSR Pillars

We've built our CSR strategy around four key pillars that align with our mission and Africa's development goals

Digital Education

Bridging the digital divide by providing access to technology education and digital skills training across African communities.

Community Empowerment

Supporting local entrepreneurs and small businesses through funding, mentorship, and access to our platform.

Environmental Sustainability

Implementing eco-friendly practices and supporting initiatives that protect Africa's rich biodiversity.

Health & Wellbeing

Improving access to healthcare information and services through our digital platform and partnerships.

Gender Equality

Promoting women's empowerment through targeted programs that address gender disparities in tech and business.

Innovation for Good

Leveraging our technology to solve social challenges and support African innovators creating positive change.

Our Impact Across Africa

1

Digital Skills Training

Small Business Support

Healthcare Access
