Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Annita's platform and services.

How do I get started with Annita?
Getting started with Annita is simple! Just download our app from the App Store or Google Play, or visit our website to create an account. Follow the registration process, verify your information, and you'll have immediate access to our core services.
What countries does Annita currently serve?
Annita currently operates in 12 African countries including Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, Rwanda, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, Ethiopia, Senegal, Ivory Coast, and Egypt. We're rapidly expanding to cover more countries across the continent.
How secure are payments on the platform?
We prioritize security above all. Annita uses bank-grade encryption for all transactions, complies with PCI DSS standards, and implements multi-factor authentication. All payment data is encrypted and we regularly conduct security audits to ensure the highest level of protection for our users.
Can I sell my products across different countries?
Yes! One of Annita's core features is enabling cross-border commerce within Africa. Our platform handles currency conversion, logistics coordination, and customs requirements, making it simple to sell your products in any country where we operate.
How does the AI integration benefit my business?
Our AI tools provide valuable insights about your customers, optimize your product listings, personalize marketing campaigns, predict market trends, and automate customer service. This helps you make data-driven decisions, increase conversion rates, and deliver exceptional customer experiences.
