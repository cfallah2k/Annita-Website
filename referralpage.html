Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our referral program below.

What counts as a successful referral?

A successful referral is when someone signs up using your unique referral link and completes at least one qualifying activity, such as:

  • Verifying their account with valid ID
  • Making their first deposit of at least $10
  • Completing their profile information
  • Making their first transaction (for business accounts)

You'll receive a notification when a referral becomes successful and the reward is credited to your account.

How do I track my referrals?

You can track all your referrals in real-time through your Annita dashboard. The dashboard shows:

  • Total referrals sent
  • Successful referrals
  • Pending referrals (signed up but haven't completed qualifying activity)
  • Total earnings from referrals
  • Reward tier progress

You'll also receive email notifications for each successful referral.

When do I receive my referral rewards?

Referral rewards are credited to your Annita wallet immediately after a referral becomes successful. There's no waiting period.

You can withdraw your referral earnings at any time, subject to our standard withdrawal processing times (usually within 24 hours).

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

No! There's no limit to the number of people you can refer. The more people you refer, the more you earn.

Plus, as you reach higher reward tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold), you'll earn more per referral. Our top referrers also qualify for special bonuses and prizes.

Can I refer people who are already using Annita?

No, the referral program only applies to new users who have never had an Annita account before. Our system automatically checks for existing accounts.

If someone tries to sign up with your referral link but already has an account, it won't count as a successful referral.

What happens if someone I referred requests a refund?

If a referred user requests a refund for their initial deposit or transaction within 14 days, the referral may be reversed and the reward deducted from your account.

This policy helps prevent abuse of the referral system. After 14 days, all referrals are considered final and won't be reversed.
