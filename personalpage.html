Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our personal services.

How do I create an AnnitaPay account?
Creating an AnnitaPay account is simple. Download our app from the App Store or Google Play, click on "Sign Up," and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide basic information and complete a quick verification process. The entire setup takes less than 5 minutes.
What are the fees for sending money?
Our fee structure is transparent and among the lowest in the industry. Domestic transfers start at just 0.5% with a cap of $5 for large transfers. International transfers range from 1-2% depending on the destination. We always show you the exact fee before you confirm any transaction.
How long do transfers take?
Most domestic transfers are instant. International transfers typically complete within 1-2 business days, depending on the destination country and banking system. Our platform provides real-time tracking so you can see exactly when your money arrives.
Is my money safe with AnnitaPay?
Absolutely. We use bank-grade security protocols, advanced encryption, and multiple authentication methods to protect your money and information. Additionally, we are fully licensed and regulated, with appropriate financial protection measures in place for our customers.
Which countries can I send money to?
AnnitaPay currently supports money transfers to over 100 countries worldwide, with a particular focus on African nations. We're continuously expanding our reach to provide more global coverage. Check the app for the most up-to-date list of supported countries.
