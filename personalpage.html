What You Get with Annita Personal

At Annita, we believe empowerment isn't just for businesses—it's for individuals too. Whether you're managing your lifestyle, finances, goals, or digital presence, Annita Personal gives you the power to do it all in one place.

Smart Wallet & Payments

Secure, seamless, and fast. Send and receive money, pay bills, and manage your finances from anywhere—online or offline—with Annitapay, our all-in-one digital payment solution.

Send App

Safely, quickly, and conveniently send money to the people who matter most to you, locally or internationally.

Lifestyle Services

Order food, shop local, book rides, access entertainment, and enjoy personalized recommendations—all from one dashboard.

Market

Discover, shop, and get your items delivered to you from your favorite online businesses—right inside your Annita experience.

Swap

Easily swap your LRD, NAIRA, and more for Dollars, Euros, or Pounds—fast, reliable, and secure currency exchange at your fingertips.

Buy Event Tickets

Conveniently find events you love and buy your tickets with just a few taps—concerts, conferences, shows, and more.

From Everyday to Extraordinary

  • Tuition Payments: Quick and easy tuition fee payments to African and international schools, with full transparency and real-time confirmations.
  • Digital Identity & Profiles: Create your personal Annita profile to connect, network, and build your online presence.
  • Personalized Dashboard: All your tools. One clean view. Track your expenses, manage subscriptions, get AI-powered insights.
  • Learning & Growth: Access expert-led content, live classes, and tutorials on money management, digital skills, and more.
  • Secure Data. Total Control: Your data is yours. With Annita, your privacy is a top priority.
