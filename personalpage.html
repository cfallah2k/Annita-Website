Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Annita's services

Is Annita free to use?

Yes! Annita's core features including messaging, calls, moments, and community features are completely free. Some premium services like AnnitaPay transfers, currency conversion, and marketplace transactions have small fees to cover operational costs.

How secure is my money with AnnitaPay?

Extremely secure. AnnitaPay uses bank-level encryption and security protocols. All transactions are monitored for fraud, and we offer purchase protection for marketplace transactions. Your funds are also kept in secure, licensed financial institutions.

Can I use Annita outside Africa?

Absolutely! While Annita is designed with African users in mind, you can use it anywhere in the world. Our currency swap feature is particularly useful for diaspora users who need to send money home or convert between currencies.

How do I find communities that match my interests?

Annita makes it easy to discover communities. You can browse by category (tech, parenting, faith, etc.), search for specific interests, or let our AI recommend communities based on your profile and activity. You can also create your own community if you don't find what you're looking for!
