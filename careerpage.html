Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about careers at Annita

What is the hiring process like at Annita?

Our hiring process typically includes an initial application review, a screening call with a recruiter, one or more interviews with the hiring team, and a final interview with leadership. We aim to move quickly while ensuring a thorough evaluation. The entire process usually takes 2-4 weeks depending on the role.

Do you offer relocation assistance?

Yes, for certain roles we offer relocation assistance to help you move to one of our office locations across Africa. The specifics depend on the role and location, but our talent team will discuss options with eligible candidates during the hiring process.

What is your remote work policy?

We embrace flexible work arrangements. Many roles are available as fully remote within Africa, while others follow a hybrid model with some days in the office. We believe in focusing on results rather than where the work happens, while also recognizing the value of in-person collaboration for certain teams.

What opportunities are there for career growth?

We're committed to helping our team members grow and develop their careers. We offer clear advancement paths, regular performance reviews, mentorship programs, and learning opportunities. Many of our leaders have been promoted from within, and we prioritize internal mobility across teams and departments.

I don't see a role that matches my skills. Can I still apply?

Absolutely! We're always looking for exceptional talent. Submit your resume to our talent pool, and our recruiters will reach out if a suitable position becomes available. You can also join our talent network to stay updated on new opportunities that match your profile.
