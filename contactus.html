Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform and services.

How do I create an account on Annita?

Creating an account is simple. Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of our website or in the mobile app. You'll need to provide some basic information and verify your email address to get started.

What payment methods does AnnitaPay support?

AnnitaPay supports various payment methods including bank transfers, debit/credit cards, mobile money, and our proprietary Annita Wallet. We're continuously adding more payment options to serve our users better.

How can I sell products on the Annita marketplace?

To sell on our marketplace, you need to register as a merchant. After account verification, you can set up your store, list products, and start selling. We provide tools for inventory management, order processing, and customer communication.

Is my data secure with Annita?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and follow strict security protocols to protect your data. Our platform is regularly audited for security vulnerabilities, and we comply with all relevant data protection regulations.

How can businesses partner with Annita?

We welcome partnerships with businesses across Africa. Please contact our Business Development team through the contact form above or email partnerships@an-nita.com with details about your business and proposed collaboration.
