Our Story

Founded in 2020, Annita Technologies began as a bold vision to bridge the digital divide in Africa. Our founder, Christopher O. Fallah, recognized the untapped potential of Africa's tech ecosystem and set out to create a platform that would empower businesses and individuals alike.

What started as a small team in Monrovia, Liberia, has grown into a pan-African technology powerhouse with operations in 15 countries and serving millions of users. Our journey has been marked by innovation, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to our mission.

Today, Annita is at the forefront of Africa's digital transformation, offering a comprehensive suite of services that include digital payments, e-commerce solutions, cloud computing, and AI-powered tools tailored for the African market.
